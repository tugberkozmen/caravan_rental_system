INFORMATION ABOUT CARAVAN CATEGORIES


1. Conventional Caravan

The majority of caravans have a single axle and are between 3 and 6.5 metres long inside. Depending on the interior layout, they can sleep between two and six people. This caravan has four solid walls and a full height rood.


2. Pop Top Caravan

This kind of caravan is similar to conventional caravan. The only difference is that the roof pop up and down which gives headroom in a small caravan.


3. Tiny Teardrop Trailer

Accommodation is normally basic with a double bed covering the entire floor. Some have simple cooking facilities and room to carry kit and equipment.


4. Fifth Wheelers

The fifth wheeler is a larger caravan that offers an enormous amount of living space. Moreover, some even have slide out sections for more room.
